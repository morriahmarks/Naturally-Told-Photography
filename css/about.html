About Me

Photography has always been about storytelling for me. Rather than creating moments, I prefer to observe and document them.

I specialize in candid photography that reflects genuine emotions, interactions, and experiences.

My editing style is minimal. I would rather spend time planning a creative shoot than relying on extensive editing afterward.

I'm not the photographer to hire if you want constant posing instructions. My goal is to help people feel comfortable enough to simply be themselves.
